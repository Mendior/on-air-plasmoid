## Pealkiri
On Air — a modern internet radio widget for Plasma 6 (worldwide search, track downloads, offline library)

## Sisu
Hi! I'd like to share my first published widget: **On Air** — an internet radio player for the Plasma panel.

**Features:**
- Modern animated UI — aurora backdrop, spinning vinyl fallback art, equalizers, LIVE & bitrate pills
- Worldwide station search: type a country ("Finland") or genre ("jazz") — powered by radio-browser.info (~50k stations). Click to preview, star to keep
- Live track info with album art lookup
- One-click track download of the currently playing song (yt-dlp), with a built-in offline "My Music" page
- Recently played history — grab a song you missed half an hour ago
- MPRIS media keys, sleep timer with fade-out, scroll-wheel volume, keyboard navigation

**KDE Store:** https://store.kde.org/p/2364623
**Source (LGPL):** https://github.com/Mendior/on-air-plasmoid

Based on Advanced Radio Player by Yuri Saurov — rebuilt with a new UI and lots of new features. Feedback very welcome!
