## Description (pikk kirjeldus)
On Air is a modern internet radio widget for your Plasma panel.
Created by Egon Greenberg (2026 edition).

FEATURES
• Modern 2026 UI — animated aurora backdrop, spinning vinyl art, cascading animations, LIVE & bitrate pills, emerald design
• Worldwide station search — type a country ("Finland") or genre ("jazz") and discover stations from the radio-browser.info catalogue (~50 000 stations). Click to preview, star to keep
• Live track info with album art (ICY metadata + iTunes/Deezer lookup)
• Stream recording — one-click REC captures the station as a bit-exact copy (original quality); scheduled recordings (once/daily/weekly) capture shows even while nothing is playing. Personal use only
• One-click track downloads at maximum quality via yt-dlp, with an offline "My Music" library page
• Recently played history — download a song you missed half an hour ago
• MPRIS media keys, sleep timer with fade-out, auto-bitrate upgrade, scroll-wheel volume, keyboard navigation

OPTIONAL DEPENDENCIES
python-requests (ICY titles), python-dbus + python-gobject (MPRIS), yt-dlp + ffmpeg (downloads), inotify-tools

AUTHOR & CREDITS
